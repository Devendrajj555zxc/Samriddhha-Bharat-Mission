About Us

Samridhha Bharat Mission is a Section 8 non-profit organization dedicated to uplifting underprivileged yet talented children across India. We design and deliver educational kits, organize creative workshops, and provide platforms for rural innovation and scientific learning.

Our Mission

To promote inclusive education, foster creativity, and support hidden talents in rural and urban India by building a network of support, awareness, and opportunities through education and handicraft-based interventions.

Our Programs

Creative Learning Kits Distribution

Providing educational materials that enhance learning experiences and spark curiosity in children across rural areas.

Art & Handicraft Workshops

Fostering creativity through hands-on art and craft activities that develop both artistic skills and practical abilities.

Science Fair & Innovation Clubs

Encouraging scientific thinking and innovation among young minds through interactive experiments and competitions.

Mentorship for Rural Talent

Connecting talented individuals with experienced mentors who guide their personal and professional development.
